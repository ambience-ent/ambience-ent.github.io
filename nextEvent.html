100% Ambience Entertainment presents Inxuba Yethemba Music Festival.

The Music Festival serves as an annual event for Cradock and surrounding areas. The Festival is a call to pay homage to Cradock community by bringing entertainment and one of their very own star studded Musicians born in the Eastern Cape and also inviting of all local entrepreneurs of Cradock to come and showcase their different businesses The Inxuba Yethemba Music Festival will be held on the 16th December 2018, in partnership with Inxuba Yethemba local Municipality To bring entertainment as service delivery to Cradock Support local and regional artists and craft workers by providing an opportunity to exhibit and market their work To stimulate enterprise development, socio - economic and social cohesion.
